# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Solar Earth Watch welcoming, respectful, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive feedback with care.
- Focus criticism on code, documentation, data handling, and project behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block contributors, or take other reasonable action to protect the project community.
